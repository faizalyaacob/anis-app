Our Booking via Mobile Apps Project (IoT)

Introduction

Nowadays, everyone uses technology to facilitate daily work. The hotel is also no exception using this technology. For example, when booking through a mobile app, the hotel can provide targeted discounts to guests booking via mobile phone, either through the app only or through the mobile or tablet version of the trivago website in addition to the app. Throughout the booking journey, guests are given this mobile price incentive through very clear value propositions, which in turn encourages conversions. This truly reflects the continued growth in mobile usage and provides great opportunities for hotel operators, who can benefit from the introduction of mobile prices.

Project Objective

1. To enhance the reservation experience of customer using mobile apps
2. To improve the knowledge of customer using the mobile apps
3. To make it easy for customer to check the room available by booking via mobile apps
4. To improve the process during hotel check in

Project Significance

1. Mobile apps are faster
2. Personalized content
3. Instant online and offline access
4. Push notification and instant updates
5. Productivity improvement and cost reduction
6. Interactive engagement
7. Increased SEO potential for the website
